Our Story

A Cup Full of
Hyderabadi Heritage

Born in 2014 in the bustling lanes of Abids, Chai Chronicles was built on one simple belief — a perfect cup of chai can carry the weight of an entire culture.

We brew our Irani chai the old way: slow simmered, full cream, steeped with tradition. Every Osmania biscuit is baked fresh at dawn. Every cup poured with care and intention. It's not just tea — it's an emotion that Hyderabadis have cherished for generations.

Today, our humble café has become a neighbourhood institution — where students debate, elders reminisce, and strangers become lifelong friends over chai that never disappoints.

— Ahmed & Fatima, Founders
